About PM-internAI

AI-powered internship recommender for every student in India.

PM-internAI is designed to help students across diverse backgrounds – rural, urban, tribal, and remote – find internships that match their skills, education, and interests.

Our goal is to make internships accessible, relevant, and aligned with the PM Internship Scheme.
